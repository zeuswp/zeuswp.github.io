Cookie Policy

Information about the use of cookies on our website

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how PulsarInteract GmbH ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website under pulsarinteract.de ("Website"). It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of these technologies.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites functional or efficient, as well as to provide information to the owners of the website.

Cookies put us in a position to identify your device when you visit our website. Cookies can perform various functions, such as efficient navigation between pages, saving your preferences and generally improving your user experience.

3. Types of Cookies

The following types of cookies can be used on our website:

3.1 Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential so that you can navigate our website and use its functions. Without these cookies, certain services that you have requested cannot be provided. They enable basic functions such as page navigation, access to secure areas of the website and storage of your shopping cart contents. These cookies do not collect information about you that could be used for marketing purposes. Necessary cookies cannot be disabled.

3.2 Analytical/Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages they visit most often and whether they receive error messages. These cookies do not collect information that directly identifies a visitor. All information collected by these cookies is aggregated and therefore anonymous. They are only used to improve the functionality of our website.

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember the decisions you have made (e.g. username, language or the region in which you are located) and offer extended, more personalized functions. These cookies can also be used to save changes you have made to the text size, font types and other adjustable parts of websites.

3.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver ads that are more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of ads in a campaign and measure the effectiveness of campaigns. They usually store the fact that you have visited a website and this information is shared with other organizations like advertisers.

5. Cookies from third parties

In addition to our own cookies, cookies from third parties can also be set on our website. These cookies are placed by third parties such as Google and Facebook to collect information about your internet usage, such as when you click on one of their ads. These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these services.

6. Cookie Management

You can decide for yourself whether you want to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser settings so that cookies are rejected if you prefer.

On our website you can manage your cookie settings through our cookie banner or the "Cookie Settings" button at the bottom of the page. Here you can select which types of cookies you want to accept.

If you decide to reject cookies, you can still use our website, but some functions may be impaired.

7. Cookies disable

Please note that blocking all cookies can have an impact on the functionality of this and many other websites. Therefore, it is recommended not to block cookies.
